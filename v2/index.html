What we do

Agriculture intelligence, built for the modern farm

Farming has always been about reading the land — but today's farms are too large and complex to manage by intuition alone. FarmOS bridges the gap between raw field data and actionable insight.

From soil sensors to satellite imagery to AI-powered yield predictions, we give farmers and agronomists the tools to make confident decisions — before the season is won or lost.

Platform Features

Every tool your operation needs

Six integrated modules that cover the full crop lifecycle — from seed to harvest.

01
Field Mapping
Visualize every plot with satellite-quality boundary maps. Draw zones, assign crops, and track field history over multiple seasons at a glance.
02
Weather Intelligence
Hyperlocal 14-day forecasts tied to each field's GPS coordinates. Frost alerts, rain probability, and wind speed overlays keep you one step ahead.
03
Yield Analytics
Compare actual vs. projected yield with historical benchmarks. Identify underperforming zones and optimize inputs for the next growing cycle.
04
Irrigation Control
Automate watering schedules based on real-time soil moisture readings. Reduce water usage by up to 30% while keeping crops in their optimal range.
05
Pest Monitoring
AI-assisted early detection of pest pressure and disease risk. Receive field-specific alerts with treatment recommendations before damage spreads.
06
Crop Rotation Planner
Multi-year rotation schedules with soil health scoring. FarmOS suggests optimal crop sequences based on nutrient history and market prices.
How It Works

From soil to insight in four steps

Setup takes less than a day. Results show up within the first week.

01
Connect your sensors
Drop FarmOS IoT sensors into your fields or integrate existing equipment. Our hardware-agnostic platform supports 40+ sensor manufacturers out of the box.
02
Map your operation
Import field boundaries from shapefiles or draw them on our satellite map. Assign crops, soil types, and irrigation zones in minutes.
03
Analyze real-time data
FarmOS processes sensor readings, weather feeds, and satellite imagery continuously. Your dashboard updates every 15 minutes with actionable alerts.
04
Optimize for next season
End-of-season reports surface what worked and what didn't — with AI recommendations for inputs, rotations, and timing to improve next year's yield.

Why FarmOS

Built by agronomists, not just engineers

1
Agronomist-designed workflows
Every feature was co-designed with working farmers and certified agronomists. We know what actually matters at planting time and harvest.
2
Works offline, syncs when ready
Cell coverage is unreliable in the field. FarmOS keeps working locally and syncs all data automatically when you're back in range.
3
No data lock-in
Your field data is yours. Export everything as CSV, shapefile, or JSON at any time. We don't hold your history hostage.
4
University research-backed
Built in partnership with the University of Nebraska's precision ag program. Our yield models are validated against real multi-year field trials.
