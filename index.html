Capabilities

Everything you need
to run the operation.

Built for the pace of real farming — from spring planting through fall harvest and everything in between.

01
Field Mapping

Visualize every field boundary and crop zone on a unified map. Overlay satellite imagery, soil type, and historical yield data in one view.

02
Yield Analytics

Track bushels per acre over time. Compare fields, seasons, and practices to identify what's working — and where gains are being left behind.

03
Smart Alerts

Get notified when soil moisture drops, weather windows open, or equipment needs attention — before small issues become expensive problems.

04
Task Scheduling

Plan planting, spraying, and harvest windows around weather forecasts. Assign tasks to equipment and operators with deadline tracking.

05
Input Cost Tracking

Log seed, fertilizer, fuel, and labor costs per field. See true cost-per-acre at harvest to inform next season's decisions with hard numbers.

06
Weather Integration

Live NOAA feeds, 10-day outlooks, and historical precipitation layered over your operation. Stop checking three apps — it's all in one place.

How It Works

From soil sample
to harvest record —
one platform.

01
Map Your Operation

Import field boundaries from a shapefile or draw them manually. Add crop type, planted date, and target yield for each zone.

02
Connect Your Data Sources

Pull in soil sensor readings, weather station data, and equipment telemetry. Manual entry supported for everything else.

03
Monitor and Act

FarmOS surfaces what needs attention — a low-moisture alert, an overdue task, a favorable spray window — so nothing slips through.

04
Record and Learn

Every action is logged. At season's end, your complete operation record is ready for analysis, tax reporting, or next year's planning.
